Events

14th August

Rehersal 4PM - 6PM

The wedding rehearsal, set in a serene garden adorned with twinkling fairy lights, begins as the sun sets, casting a golden hue over the gathering. The couple, dressed casually yet elegantly, walk hand-in-hand, practicing their aisle walk under the watchful eyes of their closest friends and family. The wedding coordinator, clipboard in hand, ensures every detail is perfect, from the music cues to the positioning of the bridal party. Laughter and light-hearted jokes fill the air, easing any nerves, as the group looks forward to the big day.

Rehearsal Dinner 6:30PM - 10:00PM

The rehearsal dinner takes place in a cozy, rustic barn, transformed into a magical space with lanterns and long, family-style tables adorned with wildflowers. A feast of comfort foods, from gourmet macaroni and cheese to slow-roasted meats, is served, encouraging a relaxed and communal atmosphere. Stories and toasts are shared, some bringing tears, others laughter, as family members and friends reminisce about the couple’s journey together. The evening concludes with a surprise slideshow of the couple's memories, leaving everyone in joyful anticipation for the wedding day.

15th August

Ceremony 3:00PM - 3:30PM

The ceremony is held at a grand, historic church, its stained glass windows casting colorful patterns across the pews filled with guests. The bride, radiant in a flowing white gown, walks down the aisle to a string quartet’s rendition of a classic love song, meeting her partner, who looks on in awe and love. The air is filled with emotion as the couple exchange heartfelt vows, their voices steady yet full of emotion, promising a lifetime of love and companionship. As they are pronounced married, the church bells chime, and guests erupt in applause, celebrating the union of two souls.

Reception 5:00PM - 12:00AM

The reception unfolds in an elegant ballroom, where crystal chandeliers twinkle above and tables are set with fine china and sparkling centerpieces. The newlyweds share their first dance, a tender and beautifully choreographed piece, under the soft glow of spotlights, drawing cheers and admiration from the onlookers. A live band plays a mix of classic hits and modern favorites, enticing guests of all ages onto the dance floor. As the night progresses, guests indulge in a decadent wedding cake and a fun-filled photo booth, capturing memories of a night filled with love, laughter, and celebration.

  Dress code

Ugh... dress codes?

CEREMONY 12pm - 6pm

Dress to Impress
Ladies are encouraged to wear elegant dresses or gowns in pastel or soft tones, complemented with subtle accessories. Gentlemen can opt for classic suits or tuxedos, paired with a tie or bow tie. Comfortable yet stylish footwear is recommended for both.

RECEPTION 7pm - 12am

Keep it Classy
Ladies are encouraged to wear elegant dresses or gowns in pastel or soft tones, complemented with subtle accessories. Gentlemen can opt for classic suits or tuxedos, paired with a tie or bow tie. Comfortable yet stylish footwear is recommended for both.

AFTER-PARTY 12am onwards

Comfy and Casual
This is the time to relax and enjoy in a more laid-back style. Ladies can go for stylish yet comfortable outfits like maxi dresses or trendy separates, and gentlemen can choose casual blazers or shirts with chinos or jeans. Comfortable shoes are a must for dancing the night away.
